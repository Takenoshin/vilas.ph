Streetwear Verification System — legitimacy checker for clothing brands



The Context

Streetwear brands need a way to verify authenticity. (This is a conceptual idea)



The Problem

Streetwear can be easily copied and sold as fakes.
Buyers want proof of authenticity to increase resale value and trust.
Brands can’t track items after they’re sold.


The Solution

A concept using an NFC HANG TAG CARD and a WEBSITE to verify product legitimacy easily.



The Exploration

Criteria QR Code Printed / Woven Label Concert Wristband / Embedded NFC Chip Separate Authentication Card (Final Choice)

Woven tags can be removed and reattached to fakes.

Printed QR codes may fade or distort after washing.

Embedded NFC in Garment can melt during laundry
