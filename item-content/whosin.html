Who's In? is a timesheet app for a private company which allows employees and admins to track their logs, post announcements, and update their details.

Started from handpicking brand colors then crafted a logo which is a bit challenging since the name is quite complicated due to inclusion of two punctuation marks. However, I was able to deliver a great identity.

Wireframes are created using Balsamiq. The web and mobile was designed using Photoshop and Illustrator.
